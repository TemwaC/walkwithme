About "Walk with me"

Lake Coleridge is a region in Canterbury, bounded by mountain ranges and includes three major rivers: the Harper, Rakaia and the Wilberforce. A spectacular region, there are many walks that have been created which are well marked and maintained. In addition to these, there are a number of walks that are not on the typical tourist's radar, and this is where Robert's local knowledge and expertise are invaluable.

Robert has lived in Lake Coleridge Village since 2013. He is an avid walker, tramper, fisherman and an experienced guide. He combines his passion for the outdoors with an extensive knowledge of the local areaâ€”including the abundant bird life and native flora and fauna that can be seen on the different landscapes. Walk with me is his business. He will take you on a fully catered, personalised walking tour of the length and difficulty level that you choose. From one day (6-hour) gentle walks along the river to four or five-day tramps in the nearby ranges, Robert will create a walking experience that best suits you.

Personalised walks

Robert's local knowledge and expertise are invaluable when it comes to walking in this region.
He is able to tailor your walk to best suit your needs: fitness levels, terrain and length of time you have available.
Because Robert knows the region, he is able to understand the vagaries of the local weather, and will ensure that your walking and tramping experience is the best possible! From dropping you off at the start of your walk and picking you up at the end to guiding you for five days through the mountains, Robert will ensure that your needs are met.

An expert photographer, Robert can also help take you to the most majestic places to get your spectacular views. His knowledge of local flora and fauna means that he can identify and talk about the local trees and plants, from finding the rare orchids to expounding on the traditional medicinal properties of native plants.
Robert will create a walking experience that best suits you.
